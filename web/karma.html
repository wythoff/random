What is Karma?

The sum of a person's actions in this and previous states of existence, viewed as deciding their fate in future existences is called Karma.
Here at AlGranth, your deeds get you Karma!

Why Karma?

At ALGranth, we believe in learning and helping others learn. Thus we came up with the idea of Karma, a reward, for all those who contribute to the community.
Give back to the community, because no one has ever lost anything by sharing.
